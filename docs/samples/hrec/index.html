Hopland REC Watershed 2 Flights, 2017

These images were collected in 2017 to map Watershed-2 for longitudinal monitoring and possible restoration planning. Watershed-2 has steep topography. In the 1960s, much of it was cleared of shrubs and oaks as part of an experiment to increase forage for sheep.
